Privy careers

Privy is one of the leading marketing automation solutions for small and mid-sized merchan
Privy is the email marketing platform for Shopify & Wix stores that need the tools & coaching to grow from $1 to $10 million in sales.

We’re the #1 reviewed app for sales in the Shopify App Store. We're used by thousands of ecommerce brands around the world to generate more than $5 billion in revenue (and counting!). Our mission is to provide the tools, education, and support ecommerce brands need to grow their online stores and build thriving businesses.

While it’s easier than ever to build an online store (thanks, Shopify!), it’s harder than ever to grow it. Specifically, most ecommerce brands have two challenges with growth:
1) Getting people to make their first purchase.
2) Getting the people who do make their first purchase to come back and buy again (and again and again).

That’s why we built Privy—to give growing ecommerce brands a leg-up against competition that’s growing by the day.

We measure our success by our customers' success, while building a brand and company that we love. We take risks, learning every day how to get better. And we always make time for life outside of work (so much that it's one of our team's core values). Whether you're the intern or the CEO, we take pride in making everyone's voices heard and keeping Privy a collaborative and inclusive place to work.
