Boundless Immigration careers

Empowering immigrants with technology and data
At Boundless, our mission is to empower immigrants to navigate the immigration system more clearly, rapidly, and affordably. We launched our first product in September 2017 exclusively targeting marriage green cards (for a U.S. citizen or green card holder applying for a green card for their foreign spouse).

We are currently building out next product, helping green card holders naturalize and become citizens. Over time, we will become the "USAA for immigrants," by addressing all applications as well as become the go-to source for all information and wraparound services to start a new life in America.
