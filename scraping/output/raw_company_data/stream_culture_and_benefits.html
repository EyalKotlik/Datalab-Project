Culture and benefits at Stream

Culture overview

Stream has a casual social culture, our team is diverse and we all have different backgrounds. Now, Stream is a team of over 130+ peers from over 35 countries across the globe. We value transparency, aim for excellence, and support each other on our way to new victories. Our team consists of the strongest talents worldwide, making Stream a great place to learn and improve your skills. When it comes to software engineering, our culture is oriented towards ownership and quality: our goal is to deliver stable software.
