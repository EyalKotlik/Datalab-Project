Culture and benefits at ChartMogul

Culture overview

We are a team of smart, fun, and hardworking people spread throughout 21 different countries! We approach our work, our customers, and each other with our core values in Excellence, Integrity, Ownership, Impact, and Empathy. We also believe in a culture of experimentation; rather than what’s trending in the industry, we choose to do what’s best for the company and we’re constantly learning how to be better.
