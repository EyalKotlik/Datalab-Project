Changing the lives of Entrepreneurs around the world
We're changing the way millions of small businesses make money, spend money and track money.

Wave delivers innovative financial services and award-winning free software to small- and micro-businesses around the world.

By improving cash flow (Payments), delivering money to employees (Payroll), helping businesses get paid (Invoicing), preparing for tax time and providing business insights (Accounting, Receipts), Wave covers the spectrum of a small business owner's financial life, and helps businesses grow and thrive.
