bloomon careers

High tech e-commerce venture looking for entrepreneurial talent
Bloomon turns the traditional flower industry on its head with a unique style, ultra fresh flowers from our growers and personal delivery to our customers. The flower sector is huge with over €50 billion in annual turnover but 99% of the sector still operates offline.

With an international team of over 80 highly motivated and ambitious people we are active in five countries. Bloomon has its main office in Amsterdam, but also operates out of Germany, Denmark, UK and Taiwan.

At bloomon we assemble our bouquets with unique flowers from all over the world. We believe that diversity creates uniqueness. This is what we translate in each of our teams as well. We offer you the opportunity to join a team of unique international people who are helping us turning the industry on its head. We all love what we do – so challenge yourself, use new approaches, step out of your comfort zone, and take initiative!
bloomon is the place where EVERYTHING you do matters!
