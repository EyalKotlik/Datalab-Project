FINDMINE careers

Award-winning automation technology for the retail industry
FindMine helps iconic brands scale their editorial point of view. We solve the content bottleneck that plagues every retail touchpoint from ecomm to marketing campaigns to clientelling apps. Our content engine uses machine learning to predict and replicate the human creative process of creating "on brand" product content that guides consumers, increases CLTV, improves margins, and saves time for the brands and retailers using FindMine's solutions. The more platforms there are, and the more personalization available, the more brands need our content engine. We work with the likes of Reebok, adidas, Perry Ellis, Cole Haan, Anine Bing, Ebay, LVMH, DXL, and more, serving over 5B pieces of content a year and counting!
