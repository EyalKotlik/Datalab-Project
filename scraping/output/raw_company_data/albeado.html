Albeado careers

Breakthrough causal AI predictions, optimizations and interventions - in real time
Albeado is at the forefront of revolutionizing Big Data analysis and Internet-scale computing technologies to make predictions on, recommend action for and analyze business impact of diverse operation and business data produced from an increasingly networked world. Our patent protected solutions transforms big data machine learning into a high confidence decision support system that allows utility operators, financial services businesses or healthcare payers and their customers to improve reliability, enhance efficiency and reduce cost of services.

We are on the lookout for talented and passionate people to work on problems that could transform BigData analytics. Join our fast growing team now and help shape the products from the start.
