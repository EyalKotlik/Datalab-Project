Evil Martians careers

Extraterrestrial product development consultancy and commercial open source team
Evil Martians is an expert team building outstanding developer tools and products. With over 100 open source projects serving millions of developers globally, we create exceptional developer experiences in everything we build.

Quality and Impact
We're merchants of quality—delivering not just code, but solutions that enhance developer productivity for years to come. We focus on sustainable, maintainable results that create lasting value for our clients.

Engineering Excellence
Our approach combines deep technical expertise with genuine care for the developer experience. We leverage 15+ years of engineering experience to craft innovative solutions, while actively contributing to the global developer community.

True Partnership
We build authentic relationships with our clients, understanding their unique challenges and goals. As active partners in their success, we question assumptions, propose innovative solutions, and improve systems beyond the immediate task.

Open Knowledge Sharing
Knowledge sharing is in our DNA. We maintain 100+ open source projects, contribute actively to the developer community, and invest in the growth of the industry through technical writing and speaking.

How We Work
Our team culture is built on proactive ownership, transparent communication, and sustainable high performance. We believe in asking questions, challenging assumptions, and leveraging collective expertise. Our async-first communication style respects everyone's time while maintaining high standards of collaboration.

Join us in our mission to build outstanding developer tools and experiences that shape the future of software development.
