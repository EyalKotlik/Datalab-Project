Culture and benefits at Scry AI

Culture overview

We at ScryAI value an open culture geared towards rapid growth through constant collaboration. Our embodied values and practices distill the beliefs, practices, and learnings of every team member of Scry AI. Whether you've worked at Scry AI for years or are relatively new, you will always find an equal place at the table. We believe in a flat, non-hierarchical work style. Our key attribute is being open to change and the ability to remain dynamic.
