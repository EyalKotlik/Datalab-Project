Vanta automates security and compliance to protect customer data build trust in businesses
Vanta automates security and compliance, starting with SOC 2, to protect customer data and build trust in internet businesses. We started Vanta in 2017 as Equifax lost every American’s social security number, Home Depot leaked its customers’ credit card numbers, and Facebook admitted it sent user data to groups trying to influence the US Presidential election.


Our goal is to demystify internet security to the average person, equipping beginners with sane defaults and growing them into security-savvy principals who can explain how their company protects customer data and why their business is worthy of your trust.


We’re a growing team, passionate about making the internet more secure by raising the standard for security and building tools to help today’s businesses surpass that bar. We’d like your help – we can’t do this alone.
