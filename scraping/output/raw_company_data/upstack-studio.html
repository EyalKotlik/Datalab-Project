Upstack Studio careers

We help non-tech entrepreneurs turn their ideas into reality
Upstack Studio is a product development agency founded in 2017. Our team is focused on building impactful web & mobile apps that help improve the lives of people around the world.

We’re not just “yet another” software house that blindly follows instructions. Everything we do here at Upstack Studio is centred around the idea of empowering non-technical entrepreneurs. We work closely with them on their game-changing ideas so they can focus on creating a positive impact.

We’ve helped companies like Daikin, Acson, Recruitopia, Hack In The Box, The Malaysian Insight and Teleme take their idea from napkin sketch to a real-shipped application.

Have a great new product idea and nobody to start building it? We’d love to help.
