We power Fintech's pioneers
WealthKernel’s vision is to become the de-facto technology platform for financial infrastructure: similar to AWS or Docker but for financial services. What inspired this vision is the quest to reduce the barriers to build digital financial applications. Many of these barriers can be found in a segment of the financial industry crippled by legacy and oligopolies: financial infrastructure.

The seamless integration of a regulatory umbrella alongside all the technology components needed to build a Fintech app make today’s value proposition unique.
