Remote benefits

Co-working stipend

We fund coworking memberships for all employees.

Home office stipend

A one-time home office stipend to order everything you need to work from home!

Bonding budgets

Every team is given a budget for quarterly virtual or IRL events and individual employees are given $50/month to “meet” with colleagues 1:1 or in small groups.

Phone/internet bill reimbursement

We'll reimburse your phone and/or internet bill up to $100 each month.

Perks and benefits

Health insurance

Muck Rack provides health, dental, vision, and disability insurance for employees and their families. We offer a select plan with 100% premium coverage for individuals and 85% coverage for dependents, as well as a range of other plans

401k

We offer eligible employees the opportunity to participate in our 401(k) plan with employer contributions.

Compensation

We offer competitive geo-neutral pay in the U.S. and review compensation at least once annually to ensure internal equity and alignment with the external market.

Fully distributed

Wellness memberships

Professional development

Virtual & in-person events
