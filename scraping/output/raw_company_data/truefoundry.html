TrueFoundry careers

TrueFoundry is a Cloud-native Machine Learning Training and Deployment PaaS on Kubernetes
TrueFoundry is a powerful LLMOps & MLOps platform that enables Machine learning teams to train and Deploy models at the speed of Big Tech with 100% reliability and scalability - allowing them to save cost and train models at lightning speed with scalability.
TrueFoundry also facilitates faster experimentation with Open Source LLMs on your cloud infrastructure while simultaneously reducing operational expenses.

TrueFoundry is backed by Sequoia, Eniac & Angels like Naval Ravikant, Anthony Goldbloom & 15+ AI & ML leaders from top tech companies.

Founders are friends for 11+ years since IIT Kharagpur days, built EntHire earlier, which they have exited to InfoEdge:

- Nikunj Bajaj: Tech lead for Conversational AI Team @Facebook that launched Portal. ML Team lead @Reflektion in the valley, built a system from scratch to 600 Mn users scale. MS from UC Berkeley and BTech, Electrical Engineering- IIT Kharagpur

- Abhishek Choudhary: Senior Staff Software Engineer @Facebook. Involved in Caching Infrastructure re-design and also Led their Battery Optimization and Videos team. One of the fastest growing engineers at FaceBook. BTech, Computer Science - IIT Kharagpur

- Anuraag Gutgutia: CEOOffice Member and Vice President, Portfolio Management @WorldQuant USA ($12Bn AUM Fund). Managed USD 600Mn in GMV for WorldQuant. ex-ITC. Investor in 23+ start-ups including AltoIRA, Dunzo, ShopKirana, SkillLync, CoScreen. BTech, Electrical Engineering -IIT Kharagpur
