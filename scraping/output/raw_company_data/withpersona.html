Persona careers

All-in-one identity platform securely built to help you serve real people
At Persona, we’re humanizing online identity. To us, this means making it easy for businesses to verify customers’ identities and offering a secure, streamlined, and personalized experience for each end-user. This helps businesses understand their customers. And most importantly, people know they are being treated as a unique person, not just another anonymous user. At Persona, what brings our team together is our desire to help our fellow teammates succeed and our motivation to make the internet a safer place.
