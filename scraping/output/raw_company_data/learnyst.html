Learnyst careers

Sell courses online securely from your own branded website & mobile apps
Learnyst is an LMS Software Company based out in Bangalore, India.

We help educators to sell their courses online from their own branded website & mobile apps. We are a "SHOPIFY for education".

Our focus is to make technology simple for educators & to help them grow.

Using simple drag & drop interface, educators can create courses, mock-tests & sell it to their students across the world.

With our powerful test engine, educators can schedule CAT, GATE, IIT-JEE, GMAT, IAS & Banking Exams online.

Our software also lets them track student performances with our indepth test analytics reports.

Lastly, educators get 100% course sales credited to their bank account. We don't charge them any transaction fees.

To summarize, we offer them an end-to end solution that helps them teach online without worrying about the technology or maintanence costs of apps.


So far, Learnyst has helped 1000+ educators to generate 10X revenue over investment and save at least 80% on online teaching platform maintenance.

On Learnyst educators have launched 50000+ courses with course sales of more than $40 million and more than 300,000 students are using Learnyst branded apps.
