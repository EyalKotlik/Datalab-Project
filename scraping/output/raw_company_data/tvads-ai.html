TVADS.ai uses AI to transform Streaming TV into a performance marketing channel
At TVAds.ai, we are building the industry’s first end-to-end AI/ML driven marketing platform that makes it easy for DTC and e-commerce brands to run performance campaigns on Streaming TV - leveraging genAI to generate dynamic TV creatives using their best brand, product & social assets and advanced ML to deliver outcomes for rapidly growing brands.

Our AI-driven platform connects the dots between performance TV creative, advanced targeting, and digital measurement to drive real performance for KPIs like website visits and purchases (CPA), as well as lift across existing search, social & digital marketing channels.
