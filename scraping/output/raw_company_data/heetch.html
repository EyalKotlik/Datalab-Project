An on-demand driver app that aims for profitability and reliability
Heetch is an urban mobility app providing the coolest ride experiences to enjoy in big cities.

We launched Heetch in 2013 because we wanted to explore the rising alternative nightlife in Parisian suburbs. Since we needed a ride, we simply created a ride-sharing app full of good vibes, music, and candy… Pretty simple, don’t you think?

Our goal was to enable a whole generation to express themselves through partying, socialising and exploring a town’s possibilities. We aimed to provide a mobility solution allowing people to move around the city in an affordable yet reliable way by turning a moment as simple as a ride into an unexpectedly positive experience.

6 years later, a few things have changed: we are now operating in 3 different countries and count more than 2 million users… Nevertheless, making each ride experience unique for passengers and fairer for drivers is still our daily challenge.
