FactoryOS to optimize manufacturing operations
Amper's FactoryOS empowers manufacturers to run world-class operations with modern software. We have first-hand experience with the frustration and complexity of adopting legacy industrial technology and we're on a mission to consumerize the manufacturing tech stack.

Our unique IoT technology enables a self-installed industrial monitoring solution that allows any kind of manufacturer to deploy Amper in minutes. We have created a modern experience for manufacturers to track, execute and improve their operations with real-time, actionable data.

Amper has connected thousands of industrial assets across hundreds of factories around the world and we are investing in the next level of growth across our GTM and product teams. We are a venture-backed startup with a distributed team and HQ in Chicago.
