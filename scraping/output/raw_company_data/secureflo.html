Cybersecurity and Privacy Services
SecureFLO provides cybersecurity and privacy consulting services to financial services, healthcare, and insurance companies as well as Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) and Platform-as-a-Service (PaaS) companies selling into those industry verticals.

SecureFLO LLC was founded in 2011 by Mr. Jyotin Gambhir after consulting for six years at Deloitte & Touche LLP. Deloitte is a “Big Four” accounting firm and one of the largest professional services firms in the world. Jyotin’s goal for SecureFLO is to offer cybersecurity and privacy consulting best practices and a standards-based approach to small and midsize companies.

SecureFLO begins every new engagement by conducting an assessment to understand your current state of security and privacy policies. Next, we build and implement a plan for your company to become compliant or certified with the standards and regulations relevant to your business.

We focus on your data and the security and privacy controls in place throughout the data lifecycle. We can deliver proof of concepts, manage projects, develop a security architecture, and help you build a long-term security strategy. SecureFLO’s services are designed to provide a comprehensive, holistic view of security. In addition to identifying your security risks, we also conduct penetration testing, vulnerability scanning, code reviews, and can train your employees so they can participate in protecting your data.

If you are a midsize or large company, we will work with your technology, development, and compliance teams. For startups and companies with limited technology and compliance staff, we can act as your Chief Information Security Officer (CISO) and design and manage your information security and technology program.

Our approach to every engagement is to “Listen, Learn, and Innovate!”. We know you understand your business and supply chain the best. SecureFLO can augment your team’s skills with our cybersecurity and privacy compliance expertise.
