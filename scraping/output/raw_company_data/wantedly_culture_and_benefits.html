Culture and benefits at Wantedly

Culture overview

Our purpose in Japanese is シゴトでココロオドル人をふやす which translates to, Increase the number of people whose hearts dance through work. Every company and individual will have their own definition of what it means to have one's heart dance at work; so what's ours? Our definition of a dancing heart: 没頭することで成果を出し、成果によって成長を実感する Bottou suru koto de seika wo dashi, seika ni yotte seichou wo jikkan suru Focus, produce results, and through those results realize your growth.
