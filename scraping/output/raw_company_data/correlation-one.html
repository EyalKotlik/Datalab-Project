Data Science for All
Correlation One is a technology company whose mission is to make data science for all. We believe that data literacy is the most important skill for the future of work. We promote data literacy through global data science competitions, rigorous data skills assessments, and enterprise-grade data science education.

We have built an expert community of 250,000+ data scientists and 500+ partnerships with leading universities and data science organizations in the US, China, UK, Canada, Ireland and Latin America. Our products and programs help data professionals develop and showcase their skills, and help enterprises attract and engage better data talent.
