Enterprise Drone Automation Platform
FlytBase, Inc. – a venture-funded technology enterprise – was incorporated in 2016 with the intent to help enterprises automate and scale their drone operations. A firm conviction in the commercial drone opportunity – coupled with deep expertise in automation software, robotics, control systems and machine intelligence – underpins the company’s creation, investor support, revenue traction and growing customer base.

FlytBase is a Silicon Valley (California, USA) company and has offices in Pune, India. With customers in North America, South America, Europe, Japan, ANZ and SE Asia, FlytBase continues to build a global footprint, driven by its intelligent, API-enabled, highly scalable software platform for UAVs.

FlytBase won the Global NTT Data Innovation Contest 2019 in Tokyo, Japan. FlytBase had graduated from Cisco LaunchPad Accelerator Program in 2017 and was awarded the TiE50 Top-Startup award by TiE Silicon Valley.
