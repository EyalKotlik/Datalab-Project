Culture and benefits at Abnormal Security

Culture overview

We’re your coworkers, not your babysitters. You know what’s best for you. We ask for excellence, innovation, and velocity from our entire team, and we know that dictating how and when you work would run contrary to those expectations. We believe culture is intentionally designed and iterative. We ask, listen, and make changes as needed. We’re growing 3x year-over-year and are committed to scaling our culture alongside our company growth! We pay at the 75th percentile (for both base salary and equity) of our competitive market, and we benchmark to higher cost of living cities...

Remote benefits

Company offsites

We have an annual company kick-off event, and a budget to support regional co-working when our team members want to get together for dinner, drinks, or in-person focus time.

Home office stipend

We offer a one-time $750 home office reimbursement to all team members to get you set up for WFH in comfort and style. This stipend can be used all at once, or over time as smaller purchases are made.

Cell phone, internet, and health & wellness reimbursements

A solid internet connection, cell phones, and overall health and wellness are must-haves for remote teams on the go, and we're happy to cover some of that cost.

Perks and benefits

Medical/dental/vision

100% coverage for employees and 50% for dependents for Medical, Dental and Vision.

One medical

Abnormal covers the cost of One Medical for all employees.

Fsa

We offer a FSA program for employees that choose to set aside pre-tax dollars for healthcare expenses.

Great parental leave

12 weeks of paid maternity or paternity leave for all employees.

Great equity packages

We offer very competitive equity (ISOs) packages for most roles, allowing employees to share in Abnormal's long-term upside..

Unlimited pto

All regular salaried team members enjoy unlimited PTO. We want team members to grow with us and a big part of that is making sure our team has the opportunity to rest and recharge. We also observe 12 holidays every year.

Virtual lunch budget

Abnormal offers a monthly virtual lunch budget for all distributed employees.

Pre-tax commuter benefits

Employees can contribute up to $265/month pre-tax for public/mass transit.
