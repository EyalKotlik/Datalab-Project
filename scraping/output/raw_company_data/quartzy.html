The world’s leading lab management solution for small- and medium-sized R&D organizations
Quartzy is the world’s leading lab management solution for small- and medium-sized R&D organizations. Customers can manage their inventory, streamline purchasing communications, and consolidate ordering all in one place.

Thousands of labs at pharmaceutical/biotechnology companies as well as hospitals hospitals and academic centers use Quartzy every day improve their requisitioning and procurement processes. Because the requisitioning process is linked to the Quartzy inventory, users know exactly where things are and when to order more.

Quartzy also adds value to the suppliers in this large market by helping them reach their customers more effectively.

This combination of a software platform plus a digital distributor results in a powerful and extremely convenient user experience that helps researchers focus on their research and accelerates the pace of scientific discovery.
