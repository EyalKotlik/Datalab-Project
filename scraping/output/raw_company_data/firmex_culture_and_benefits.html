Culture and benefits at Firmex

Perks and benefits

Competitive benefits

We take care of our employees with a comprehensive benefits package. Need new glasses or a massage? We’ve got you covered.

Snacks & drinks

Free breakfast essentials, snacks, fresh fruit, coffee & juice. And a big lunch room to get away from your desk and eat with your coworkers.

Dog-friendly office

At Firmex, every day is take your dog to work day. Dogs are a part of our culture, and we’re always on the lookout for new “Fur-mex” mascots!

Team-building events

From game nights, bowling, and the Firmex World Series of Softball and Poker to our annual holiday party, there’s something for everyone.
