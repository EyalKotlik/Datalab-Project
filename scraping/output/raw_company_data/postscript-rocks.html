SMS marketing automation for ecommerce stores (FULLY REMOTE)
Postscript is redefining marketing for e-commerce companies. By introducing SMS as an entirely new channel for e-commerce stores to engage, retain, and convert their customer base, thousands of customers are are seeing huge ROI with Postscript. 

We're fully-remote across North America. We have a highly entrepreneurial culture and are growing rapidly. We work hard and enjoy doing so.

We're a product-first company. But more than anything else, our competitive advantage is that we care—a lot—like, we really really care. This shows in many ways, like responding to customers at all hours, selling fairly and honestly, and in how we've built the Postscript product.
